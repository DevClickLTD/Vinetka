# avtovia.bg — Electronic Vignette for Bulgaria Online

> avtovia.bg is a licensed platform for purchasing electronic vignettes for Bulgaria, checking vignette validity and managing your vignette profile. Fast, secure, 24/7 accessible.

**Heritage note:** avtovia.bg is the successor to the informational website Vinetka.bg and now adds the ability for users to purchase a vignette online themselves.

avtovia.bg is operated by Digital Policy Hub. Vignette sales are handled by "Digitoll Smart Infrastructure" JSC (Digitoll) — a National Service Provider licensed by the Bulgarian Road Infrastructure Agency (API) since 2019. Digitoll is a subsidiary of the American Concord, Inc.

The platform serves over 10,000 satisfied customers with 99.9% uptime and 24/7 online access. Accepts Mastercard and VISA card payments. Available in 12 languages.

## Important Facts About Bulgarian Electronic Vignettes

- Electronic vignette mandatory for all vehicles up to 3.5t on Bulgarian national road network since 2019 (Road Act).
- No physical sticker issued — fully digital and linked to vehicle registration number.
- Enforcement: Automatic toll cameras operating 24/7.
- Fine without vignette (passenger car): 153.30 € / 300 BGN; compensatory fee (paid within 14 days): 35.78 € / 70 BGN.
- Registration number must be entered exactly as on the vehicle document (capital Latin letters, no spaces or dashes).
- Technical error application (wrong vignette) can be submitted via the official API document.
